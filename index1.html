Parabola

About

  1. The dynamic content demonstrates the parabola and its parts.
  2. The learner can observe and calculate different parts of the parabola.
  3. This activity will help learners to understand and retain knowledge about parabola and its parts like vertex, focus, Axis of symmetry, Directrix, intercepts, focal length, and eccentricity.

Instructions

  1. Learner can see the parabola and its parts.
  2. The buttons provided in the right top corner will help the student to view different types and forms of the parabola.
  3. Learner can drag the values of a, b, c, p, h, and k by using the sliders provided at the bottom.
  4. Once learners drag any of the values, they can see the change in the parabola as well as the change in the values of its parts.
